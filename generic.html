REMIX

The project started by questioning the new trends on social media and all the hype around all the Web3 developments. Seeig all this trends and users interact on a daily basis mainly on Twitter made us realize that every account on Twitter is, in it's own way, a remix.

By creating this fake user we were able to interact and experience this communnities, learn from them, and copy their behaviors, every tweet, retweet, like, and comment is taking inspiration from other sources on different Web3 topics. The ultimate goal is to appropriate all this content that people upload and make it ours, so that in the end we can becoma a part of this type of communities and explore them from the inside.
